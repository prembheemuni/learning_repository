## Computer Architecture

`Storage units`

- 1 bit - 0/1
- 1 byte - 8 bits
- 1 kilo byte - 1024 bytes
- 1 Mega byte - 1024 KB
- 1 GB - 1024 MB
- 1 TB - 1024 GB


## `Storage types`

`DISC Storage (ROM)`

- Primary storage for the computer
- non-volatile memory , in the absence of power the files remain safe
- SSD/HDD
